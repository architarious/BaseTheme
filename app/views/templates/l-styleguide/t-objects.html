Alerts

Alerts are simple components often posted at the tops of pages. They're often used to highlight interaction results or crucial information.

@@include('objects/_alerts.html')

Chips

A chip is a simple component typically consisting of a title, a short message and an avatar. It's frequently wrapped in a link, but not always. You generally see them used to reference individuals.

Tiles

A tile is similar to a thumbnail, but different in that it only contains a brief string of text that functions as a CTA. They are often displayed in sidebars, scrollers and grids.

@@include('objects/_tile.html')

Media Objects

Media objects are a collection of components that are used to give context to featured content links. You often see them used to reference blog posts or important promotions. Think of them as a chip, but with meta content.

.spotlight

Spotlights are often used to highlight featured content with multiple calls to action. You often see them displayed as a row on a homepage, but they can also be used in column formats on a backpage.

Navigation Lists

Four different variations of navigational lists exist for a variety of different purposes. In order to recolor them use the navList--recolor($textColor, $shadowColor, $lightColor) mixin within the UL styles of your navList.

.navList

Use the standard .navList class when you need any horizontal navigation.

@@include('objects/_navlist.html') @@include('objects/psuedo/_navlist.html')

.navList--vertical

Use the .navList--vertical component for secondary navigations or any navigation with multiple levels of hierarchy.

@@include('objects/_navlist--vertical.html') @@include('objects/psuedo/_navlist--vertical.html')

.navList--simple

Use the .navList--simple class when you need a secondary navigation but don't want the extra lines and padding present in .navlist--vertical.

@@include('objects/_navlist--simple.html') @@include('objects/psuedo/_navlist--simple.html')

.navList--condensed

Use the .navList--condensed class when you need a hierarchical navigation list in a tight spot; like in a footer.

@@include('objects/_navlist--condensed.html') @@include('objects/psuedo/_navlist--condensed.html')
